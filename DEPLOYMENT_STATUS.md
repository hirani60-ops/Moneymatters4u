## 🚀 QUICK DEPLOYMENT (5 MINUTES)

### **Step 1: Open Streamlit Cloud**
```
https://share.streamlit.io
```

### **Step 2: Deploy**
1. Click "Sign in" → "Sign in with GitHub"
2. Click "New app"
3. Select: `hirani60-ops/Moneymatters4u`
4. Main file: `app.py`
5. Click "Deploy"
6. **Wait 2-3 minutes**

### **Step 3: Configure Secrets**
1. Click ⋮ → Settings → Secrets
2. Paste:
```toml
AZURE_API_ENDPOINT = "your-endpoint"
AZURE_API_KEY = "your-key"
MODEL_NAME = "Phi-4"
```
3. Save & wait 60 seconds

### **Step 4: Test**
- Visit: https://moneymatters4u.streamlit.app/
- Click "🎮 Load Demo Data"
- Explore all 8 tabs
